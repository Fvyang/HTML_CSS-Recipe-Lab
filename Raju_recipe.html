Mix Vegetable Fry

Whenever I'm in a good mood this is the recipe use to cook and enjoy withy my family in weekends. It is super delicious and nutricious dish with loaded protien and fiber in a simple dish. Also is very simple method to cook in very short time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1/2 spoon black pepper
  • 2 cut garlic clove
  • 1 medium sized red onion
  • ½ cups olive oil
  • 1 tea spoon oyster sauce
  • 1 large size carrot
  • 1 medium sized broccoli
  • 4 oz green peas
  • half pound inch square cut tofu

Instructions:

  1. Heat the wok pan and put the oil. Once the oil get heated, stir & fry the garlic for a minute. Once garlic get brown add the vegetables you have and stir for minutes. Cover the pan with lid and contiune to cook in medium heat for another 5 minutes. stir couple times.
  2. Add salt and pepper and oyster sayce and stir until all mixed in
  3. Remove pan from heating and do not cover the lid
  4. Your favorate dish is ready
  5. Let cool and then enjoy a great meal in your favorate bowl!
