Mix Vegetable Fry

Whenever I'm in a good mood this is the recipe I use to cook my favorate dish and enjoy with my family in weekends and friends over. It is super easy,delicious and nutricious healthy dinner loaded with protien and fiber. Also is very simple method to cook in very no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1/2 spoon black pepper
  • 2 cut garlic clove
  • 1 medium sized red onion
  • ½ cups olive oil
  • 1 table spoon oyster sauce
  • 1 large size carrot
  • 1 medium sized broccoli
  • 4 oz green peas
  • half pound square cut tofu

Instructions:

  1. Heat the wok pan and put the the olive oil. Once the oil get hot, stir & fry the garlic for a minute. Once garlic get brown add the vegetables you have that was cut and ready.
  2. Add salt and pepper and oyster sayce and stir until all mixed in and Stir vegetables every couple minutes about 6 minutes. Cover the pan with its lid and continue cooking in medium heat for another 3 minutes.
  3. Now, your favorate dish is ready
  4. Let it cool for couple minutes and enjoy a great meal in your favorate bowl!
